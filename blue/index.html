Knowledge preserved, research accelerated

People come and go, but their experience shouldn't walk out the door with them. LabMatters turns scattered conversations, protocols, and documents into a living, searchable knowledge base your group can rely on for years.

For lab members

Learn faster and stop reinventing the wheel — find the answer someone already worked out, and spend more time on the research itself.

For lab administrators

Cut repetitive training and redundant questions. Keep procedures, inventories, and safety information organised in one place.

For PIs & managers

Secure your group's collective knowledge, reduce incidents, and give your team a centralised, searchable home for everything that matters.

Another solution from our group

PRISM — turn scattered documents into structured knowledge

Years of know-how end up locked inside PDFs, Word files, manuals, and old SOPs. PRISM digitalises them — automatically converting, enriching, and tagging your documents so they're ready to live and be searched inside LabMatters.

PRISM™

From raw files to enriched, searchable knowledge

Converts PDFs, Word documents, manuals and SOPs into clean, structured Markdown.
Automatically extracts safety warnings, equipment specs and material lists.
Tags and standardises content so everything is consistent and easy to find.
Publishes straight into your LabMatters space — no manual re-formatting.

PDF / Word  →  PRISM  →  structured, enriched, searchable document.
