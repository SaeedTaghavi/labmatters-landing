Built for the Realities of Research Lab Life

Whether you manage a core facility, university research group, or industrial R&D lab — LabMatters has a space for every challenge you face.

Protocol & SOP Sharing

Post and refine standard operating procedures with the community. Find tested protocols from peers who've solved the same problems.

Equipment Troubleshooting

Get help diagnosing instrument errors, share maintenance tips, and tap into collective experience from thousands of lab professionals.

Safety & Compliance

Discuss COSHH assessments, risk management, and regulatory requirements. Share frameworks that keep your team safe and compliant.

Inventory & Procurement

Explore strategies for reagent management, vendor evaluation, cold-chain logistics, and keeping budgets on track.

People & Training

Share onboarding checklists, induction materials, and mentoring approaches for new lab members and students.

Quality & Accreditation

Navigate ISO 17025, GLP, and other quality standards with colleagues who've been through the process.
