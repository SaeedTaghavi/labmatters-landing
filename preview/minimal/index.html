We are LabMatters

The Problem With Lab Management

Running an experimental research lab is one of the most demanding jobs in science — but lab managers have historically worked in isolation. Equipment failures, compliance questions, procurement headaches, and onboarding challenges are solved again and again in silos, with no shared knowledge base to draw from.

How is LabMatters different?

LabMatters is a Discourse-based peer community built specifically for experimental research lab management. We connect lab managers across institutions and disciplines so that hard-won practical knowledge gets shared — not lost. Ask questions, contribute answers, and build a professional network that actually understands your work.

Why LabMatters?

We're able to serve your lab management needs better than a general forum ever could.

Protocols & SOPs

Share and refine standard operating procedures with peers who have tested them in real lab environments.

Equipment & Troubleshooting

Get help diagnosing instrument errors and tap into collective maintenance experience from thousands of professionals.

Safety & Compliance

Navigate COSHH, risk assessments, and quality accreditation with colleagues who have been through it before.
